Install App

To run this game in full screen without the browser bar:

1. Tap the Share button in the bottom bar (square with an up arrow).

2. Scroll down and select
"Add to Home Screen"

How to Play

Gather your friends around the screen and follow these simple steps:

  1. Everyone places one finger on the screen.
  2. Wait for the cosmic mist to sync its rhythm.
  3. The last illuminated expanding aura is the winner!
