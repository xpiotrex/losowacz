Install App

To run this game in full screen without browser bars:

1. Tap the Share button in the bottom navigation bar (square with an arrow pointing up).

2. Scroll down the menu and choose
"Add to Home Screen"

How to Play

Gather your friends around the screen and follow these simple steps:

  1. Everyone places one finger on the interface.
  2. Wait a moment for the cosmic aura to synchronize its pulse.
  3. The chosen illuminated expanding mist reveals the winner!
